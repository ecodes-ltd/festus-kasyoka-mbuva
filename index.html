About Festus Kasyoka Mbuva

Festus Kasyoka Mbuva is an aspiring MP for Mbooni sub-county, dedicated to fostering growth through education, sports, and community projects. With a vision for a stronger, more united Mbooni, Festus seeks to make a lasting difference.

Community Initiatives

  • Education Sponsorships: Supporting needy students to achieve their educational dreams.
  • Sports & Tournaments: Engaging youth through sports, including annual tournaments and community events.
  • Development Projects: Active participation in community clean-ups, infrastructure improvements, and local business support.
